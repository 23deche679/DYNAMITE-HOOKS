Our Story

From humble beginnings to sustainable impact

Weaving Sustainability into Every Stitch

Founded in 2018, Dynamite Hooks began as a passion project in a small apartment. What started as a hobby quickly transformed into a mission to revolutionize the crochet industry with sustainable practices.

We believe that beautiful creations shouldn't cost the earth. That's why we source only eco-friendly materials and partner with artisans who share our commitment to ethical production.

Every Dynamite Hooks product tells a story - of environmental consciousness, artisanal skill, and timeless design that transcends seasons.

Our Sustainable Practices

Committed to people and planet

Eco Materials

We use only organic, recycled, and biodegradable materials that leave minimal environmental footprint.

Fair Wages

Every artisan receives fair compensation and works in safe, ethical conditions.

Carbon Neutral Shipping

All shipments are carbon offset through our reforestation partnerships.

Zero Waste

We repurpose all yarn scraps into new products or donate them to community craft programs.
